What is the Hult Prize?

The Hult Prize Foundation transforms how young people envision their own possibilities as leaders of change in the world around them. With a US$ 1,000,000 global startup prize as its anchor activity, the Hult Prize has brought impact-focused programs, events and trainings to over a million students globally, creating a pathway for youth everywhere to take action to build a better world.

Why you should participate?

  • Certificates for all participants signed by former US President Bill Clinton and Ahmad Ashkar, CEO of Hult Foundation
  • Mentorship and incubation opportunities from a renowned panel of investors and industry experts
  • A chance to represent our college in the prestigious Hult Regional Finals held in one of six countries around the globe
  • An unparalleled learning experience, while solving some of the greatest challenges that plague humanity

This Year's Theme

The Hult Prize organization has officially announced this year's problem statement: "An idea with a global impact and alignment with at least one Sustainable Development Goal."

This year, you can pitch any idea you have for a social enterprise, as long as it is world-changing and aligned with at least one of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

The clock is ticking, so pull your socks up and start working on your B-plan.

Flow of the Competition

1

2
